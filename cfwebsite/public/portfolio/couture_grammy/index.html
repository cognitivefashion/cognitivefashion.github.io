AI for Fashion

This IBM Research AI project aims to build a suite of APIs for the fashion industry primarily leveraging deep learning, natural language processing and computer vision.

The usecases are targeted towards end consumers, online retailers, buyers, merchandisers and designers.

Grammys 2018

AI analyses fashion at Grammys 2018

The Recording Academy and IBM worked together to integrate Watson’s artificial intelligence into the red-carpet experience at the 60th Annual Grammy Awards. During the five-hour celebrity procession, Watson used the Cognitive Fashion APIs to understand styles, learn about this year’s fashion trends and compare them to those of previous years.
